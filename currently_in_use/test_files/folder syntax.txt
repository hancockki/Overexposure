Folder locations defined by:
	Do they satisfy assumption 1 (share max two rejecting nodes)
	Type of graph (Barabasi-Albert, Erdos-Renyi, Watts-Strogatz)
	Number of Nodes (100, 500, 1000, 2000, other [aka misc.])

NSA1 = Not Satisfy Assumption 1
	Means: not satisfy max of two shared rejecting nodes

SA1 = Satisfy Assumption 1
	Means: satisfy max of two shared rejecting nodes
